üìãAmazon's Strict Rules for Main Photos

Amazon wants the main picture to have a PURE WHITE BACKGROUND with NO text, logos, borders, watermarks, or other drawings!

ü§îWhy So Strict?

Amazon wants a simple, high-quality shopping page. They want buyers to quickly and clearly see the product itself, without distracting ads. This helps build trust and keeps selling fair.

üß† Main Photo: Stand Out Smart

(Following Rules!)

Yes, on Ozon and Wildberries, we also had limits. We always looked for ways to stand out. On Amazon, even with strict rules, there are some smart tricks to make your main photo better without breaking rules:

üé≠Depth and Realism

The best way is to take a good photo with natural shadows or reflections. Aim for a sense of depth and volume. Your product shouldn't look "flat" on white. Good lighting that creates soft shadows can greatly improve how your product looks and catch the eye.

üè∑Ô∏è"Tricks" with Product Design

Some sellers try to add new text or infographics with important info (for example "gluten-free," "organic," or "500ml" etc) onto the product's label in the photo (like a mockup). Buyers will notice this, and Amazon's checks will see it as part of the product. This is not a rule break because the text is part of the product's design.

üß† More Smart Tricks

üí°Contrast and Light

Sometimes, just a small change to contrast or better light can make your product look "juicier" and stand out on the white background. But be careful not to make it look fake.

üì¶Part of Packaging

For food, this can be useful. For example, for nori sheets, you can show a photo where the nori sheets are coming out of an open pack. This helps buyers better see the product inside and its texture. Such a photo can really stand out.

‚ö° IMPORTANT: After changing your main photo, always check your CTR and do A/B tests. Amazon lets you test different pictures to see what works best. You don't have to change all photos at once; just try one to test your idea and find the best option.
